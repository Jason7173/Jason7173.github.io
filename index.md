My name is **Jaehyun Lee**, and I am a M.S student in the [Data Artificial Intelligence (DAI) Lab](https://da.snu.ac.kr/) at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. [Jaemin Yoo](https://jaeminyoo.github.io/) and Prof. [Kijung Shin](https://kijungs.github.io/). My research interests cover representation learning on structured data such as graph, time-Series, and tabular data, together with its application to real-world situations.
